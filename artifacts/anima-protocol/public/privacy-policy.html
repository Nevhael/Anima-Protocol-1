Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Echoes of Eden Inc. ("we," "us," "our") operates the Anima Protocol platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Key Privacy Commitments:
  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not use conversations to train models without consent
  • You control what emotional data is recorded
  • You can request data deletion anytime

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Full name (optional)
  • Date of birth (for age verification)
  • Account preferences

2.2 Conversation and Memory Data

  • Chat messages and responses
  • Emotional states recorded in memory system
  • Relationship progression data
  • Scenario preferences
  • User-generated scenarios and content

2.3 Usage Information

  • Login times and frequency
  • Features accessed
  • Time spent in conversations
  • Device information (browser, OS)
  • IP address

2.4 Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store full credit card numbers. See Stripe's Privacy Policy for payment data handling.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide Platform features and personalize experiences
  • Memory Systems: To create persistent emotional memory and relationship tracking
  • Account Management: To verify age, manage accounts, and send service notifications
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns (anonymized) and improve features
  • Safety: To detect abuse, enforce Terms of Service, and prevent illegal activity
  • Communications: To send product updates, security alerts, and customer support

4. Data You Can Control

4.1 Memory and Emotional Data

You can:

  • Toggle emotional memory recording on/off in Settings
  • View all recorded memories about you in your Dashboard
  • Delete specific memories
  • Clear all emotional data for a conversation

4.2 Data Deletion Rights

You can request deletion of:

  • Your entire account and all associated data
  • Specific conversation histories
  • Memory records
  • User-generated scenarios

Submit deletion requests to: privacy@echoesofedenai.com

We will delete data within 30 days, except where required to keep for legal/safety reasons.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Service Providers: Stripe (payments), hosting providers (with data processing agreements)
  • Legal Obligation: If required by law, court order, or to prevent illegal activity
  • Safety: If necessary to protect safety of users or property

6. AI Training and Data

Important: We do not use your conversations to train models by default.
  • Your chat messages are NOT used to improve our AI without opt-in consent
  • You can opt-in to allow anonymized conversation data for model improvement
  • If you opt-in, personally identifiable information is removed first
  • You can withdraw consent anytime in Settings

7. Data Security

We implement:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Incident response procedures

However, no system is perfectly secure. Report security issues to: security@echoesofedenai.com

8. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data deleted within 30 days (except legal holds)
  • Backups: Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days
  • Legal Holds: Data retained if required by law

9. International Data Transfer

Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries. By using the Platform, you consent to data transfer and processing in these locations. If you are in the EU, you have additional GDPR rights (see Section 10).

10. GDPR and CCPA Rights

10.1 For EU Users (GDPR)

You have rights to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion (right to be forgotten)
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Data Portability: Get your data in a portable format
  • Object: Opt out of certain processing

10.2 For California Users (CCPA)

You have rights to:

  • Know: What personal information we collect and use
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-Out: Prevent data sales (we don't sell, but you can opt out of sharing)

Submit requests to: privacy@echoesofedenai.com

11. Cookies and Tracking

We use:

  • Session Cookies: For authentication and basic functionality
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings
  • Analytics (optional): To understand usage patterns (opt-in)

You can disable cookies in your browser, though some Platform features may not work.

12. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

13. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be announced in-app. Your continued use constitutes acceptance.

Data Requests: privacy@echoesofedenai.com
Complaints: dpo@echoesofedenai.com
