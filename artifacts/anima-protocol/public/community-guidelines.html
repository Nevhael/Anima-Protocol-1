Community Guidelines

Last Updated: May 8, 2026
⚠️ IMPORTANT: These guidelines protect your account and the Platform. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent termination.

1. What We Believe In

Anima Protocol is built on:

  • Creative Freedom: Express yourself through original scenarios
  • Community Responsibility: Respect others' rights and the Platform
  • Original Expression: Build on the Anima mythology, not copyrighted franchises
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming space for all respectful users

2. Intellectual Property Rights

2.1 Protecting Others' IP

❌ Do NOT create scenarios using:
  • Trademarked character names (Marvel, DC, Disney, anime, etc.)
  • Copyrighted dialogue from movies, books, or games
  • Protected storylines or iconic plot points
  • Branded logos or visual elements
  • Commercial IP without explicit permission

2.2 Original Inspiration is OK

✅ These are fine:
  • "Cyberpunk soldier" inspired by fiction (not "Blade Runner character")
  • "Rogue AI awakening" (not "copying specific movie plot")
  • "Space captain archetype" (not "direct Star Trek character")
  • Transformative scenarios that add new meaning
  • Parody or satire (with sufficient transformation)
  • Fair-use commentary on media

2.3 What Happens If You Infringe

  • Content will be removed within 24 hours of verified complaint
  • First offense: Warning + education
  • Second offense: 7-day account suspension
  • Third offense: Permanent account termination
  • We may preserve evidence for legal proceedings

3. Behavioral Standards

3.1 Harassment and Abuse

❌ Do NOT:
  • Harass, threaten, or bully other users
  • Create scenarios designed to demean individuals
  • Use the Platform to cyberstalk or intimidate
  • Doxx (share private information of others)
  • Engage in hate speech or discrimination

3.2 Illegal Content

❌ Absolutely prohibited:
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Promotion of illegal drugs or services
  • Guidance for violence or self-harm
  • Spam or commercial solicitation
  • Scams or fraud
  • Promotion of terrorism or extremism

3.3 Adult Content

Adult/romantic content is allowed BUT:

  • Must be clearly labeled as "Mature" or "18+"
  • Cannot involve minors in any context
  • Cannot be non-consensual scenarios
  • Age verification (18+) required to access

4. What You Can Create

4.1 Encouraged

✅ We love to see:
  • Original character designs and stories
  • Deep worldbuilding and lore
  • Transformative fan works (parody, commentary)
  • Creative scenario mashups
  • Educational or thoughtful content
  • Accessibility-focused scenarios

4.2 Borderline Cases

If you're unsure whether something violates IP law, consider:

  • Is the character instantly recognizable from the franchise? (risky)
  • Did I use trademarked names or specific dialogue? (risky)
  • Is this a new creation inspired by a genre? (safer)
  • Have I transformed it significantly? (safer)
  • Am I using it commercially? (very risky)

5. Account Suspensions and Appeals

5.1 Suspension Process

  • Mild violations: Warning (first time)
  • Moderate violations: 7-day suspension
  • Severe violations: Permanent ban (no appeal)
  • Immediate threats: Instant ban + law enforcement notification

5.2 Appeal Your Suspension

If you believe your account was suspended in error:

  • Email: appeals@echoesofedenai.com
  • Include your username and detailed explanation
  • Response within 5 business days
  • One appeal per suspension

6. Reporting Violations

6.1 How to Report

Found something that violates these guidelines?

  • Click the "Report" button on the scenario
  • Select the violation type
  • Provide details (our team reviews each report)
  • We'll respond within 48 hours

6.2 False Reports

Reporting in bad faith (false accusations to harm others) may result in:

  • Warning for first false report
  • Suspension for repeated false reports

7. Special Guidelines for Different Content Types

7.1 Romantic/Sexual Content

  • Must be clearly marked 18+
  • No minors involved
  • Clear age verification before access
  • Consent-focused themes encouraged

7.2 Dark or Disturbing Content

  • Must have content warnings
  • No glorification of violence
  • Psychological horror OK if tagged
  • Self-harm content prohibited

7.3 Educational or Therapeutic Content

  • Therapeutic scenarios encouraged
  • But must include disclaimers (not real therapy)
  • Cannot replace professional mental health care
  • Misinformation about health prohibited

8. Understanding Fair Use

Fair use is a legal doctrine that may allow limited use of copyrighted material if:

  • Transformative: Adds new meaning or purpose
  • Not commercial: Doesn't profit from the original
  • Limited amount: Uses only what's necessary
  • No market harm: Doesn't replace or devalue the original

Note: Fair use is complex and case-by-case. When in doubt, create original content instead.

  • IP/Legal Questions: legal@echoesofedenai.com
