DMCA TAKEDOWN POLICY
Echoes of Eden Inc. - Anima Protocol
Last Updated: May 8, 2026

================================================================================
REPORTING INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY INFRINGEMENT
================================================================================

If you believe content on the Anima Protocol platform infringes your copyright,
trademark, or other intellectual property rights, please submit a DMCA takedown
notice to:

EMAIL: legal@echoesofedenai.com
SUBJECT: [DMCA TAKEDOWN] - [Your Name]

================================================================================
REQUIRED INFORMATION
================================================================================

Your takedown notice MUST include:

1. YOUR CONTACT INFORMATION
   - Full name
   - Address
   - Email address
   - Phone number

2. IDENTIFICATION OF THE COPYRIGHTED WORK
   - Title of the copyrighted work
   - Description of the work
   - Date of creation/publication
   - Proof of ownership (copyright certificate, license agreement, etc.)

3. IDENTIFICATION OF INFRINGING CONTENT
   - Specific scenario URL or content ID
   - User who created/posted the content
   - Exact location within the Platform where infringement occurs
   - Clear description of how it infringes

4. GOOD FAITH STATEMENT
   "I have a good faith belief that the use of this content is not authorized
   by the copyright holder, its agent, or the law, and that this notice is
   accurate under penalty of perjury."

5. YOUR SIGNATURE
   - Handwritten or electronic signature acceptable
   - Type your full name as signature

6. OPTIONAL BUT HELPFUL
   - Screenshots or copies of infringing content
   - Documentation of your copyright/trademark registration
   - Previous takedown notices (if applicable)

================================================================================
RESPONSE TIMELINE
================================================================================

Upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice, Echoes of Eden Inc. will:

1. Within 24 hours: Acknowledge receipt of the notice
2. Within 5 business days: Review and assess the claim
3. Within 10 business days: 
   - Remove infringing content if claim is valid
   - Notify the content creator (counter-notice opportunity)
   - Notify you of action taken

================================================================================
REPEAT INFRINGER POLICY
================================================================================

Users who repeatedly upload infringing content will:

- First violation: Warning
- Second violation: 7-day account suspension + content removal
- Third violation: Permanent account termination
- Severe/commercial violations: Immediate termination

================================================================================
COUNTER-NOTICE PROCEDURE
================================================================================

If your content was removed and you believe it was removed in error, you may
file a counter-notice:

EMAIL: legal@echoesofedenai.com
SUBJECT: [DMCA COUNTER-NOTICE] - [Your Username]

Your counter-notice must include:

1. Identification of the removed content
2. Declaration under penalty of perjury that:
   - You are the content creator, or
   - You have authorization from the copyright holder
3. Explanation of why you believe the content does NOT infringe
4. Your contact information
5. Your signature

After receipt of a valid counter-notice:
- We will notify the claimant
- If they don't file suit within 10 days, we restore the content
- Disputes are resolved through courts (we don't arbitrate)

================================================================================
FAIR USE CONSIDERATIONS
================================================================================

Content may qualify for fair use protection if it:
- Is transformative (new meaning, message, or expression)
- Doesn't substitute for the original work commercially
- Uses only the amount needed for the purpose
- Doesn't harm the original's market value

Examples of likely fair use:
✓ Parody or satire
✓ Transformative fan works
✓ Critical commentary
✓ Educational use

Examples of likely infringement:
✗ Direct character replication with trademarked names
✗ Dialogue copied from copyrighted works
✗ Commercial use of copyrighted material
✗ Substantial copying of plot/characters

================================================================================
DISPUTES AND LEGAL ACTION
================================================================================

Disagreements about takedown notices are resolved through:

1. Direct communication (we encourage this)
2. Legal process (courts decide disputed claims)
3. NOT mediation or binding arbitration

Echoes of Eden Inc. is NOT a judge of copyright disputes—we defer to courts.
